1. Program Overview: TiE Women 2026

The TiE Women Program is a 6-month global accelerator designed to support women-led startups through mentorship, investor access, and a global pitch competition. 5. Recommendations for Application

Pre-Application Actions (Critical)

  1. Verify/Establish Female Founder Status: Confirm the founding team structure meets the 33% female equity requirement. If needed, formalize a co-founder arrangement with a qualified woman who has genuine involvement in the business.
  2. Register the Business: Ensure formal business registration is complete before the June 15 deadline.
  3. Document Fundraising Activity: Prepare evidence of active fundraising efforts (investor conversations, pitch deck distribution, grant applications).

Application Strategy

  1. Lead with Impact Story: Emphasize the survivor transformation journey - from crisis to entrepreneur. This narrative aligns perfectly with TiE Women's mission.
  2. Quantify the Problem: Use the compelling statistics (37.1% GBV rate, 65% period poverty, zero local manufacturers) to establish urgency.
  3. Highlight the Integrated Model: Stress that the three-pillar approach is a genuine innovation in social enterprise design.
  4. Address Scale Thoughtfully: Frame the CHE network as a "franchise-like" distribution model that enables rapid scaling without proportional capital requirements.
  5. Emphasize Unit Economics: 50% gross margin, break-even by Month 8, and 289% ROI demonstrate business viability.

Pitch Preparation

  1. Develop a 3-Minute Pitch: Focus on problem → solution → traction → ask → impact
  2. Prepare Visual Aids: Use the survivor journey infographic and impact metrics dashboard
  3. Anticipate Questions: Be ready to discuss scaling challenges, competitive threats, and sustainability
  4. Practice with Mentors: Seek feedback from social enterprise and African market experts
